📸 Report a Waste Issue

Submit a waste problem in your barangay. Your report creates an official tracked ticket visible to your LGU officer.

🌿 Earn 15 Eco-Points when your report is validated by an LGU officer.
0 / 300

📍 Your GPS location will be automatically tagged to this report upon submission.

🌿 +15 Eco-Points will be credited to your wallet once this report is validated by your LGU officer.

How It Works

1
Submit your report. Describe the waste problem and your location. Your GPS coordinates are auto-tagged.
2
LGU officer reviews it. Your report appears on the CENRO dashboard as an open ticket within minutes.
3
Collector is assigned. An assigned garbage collector receives the report on their app interface.
4
You get notified. A push notification updates you every time your report status changes.
5
Earn 15 Eco-Points. Points are credited automatically after your report is validated.

Report Status Guide

Open Submitted, awaiting review
Acknowledged Seen by LGU officer
In Progress Collector assigned and on the way
Resolved Issue addressed and closed
